About ExpressionArt


ExpressionArt's aim is to rehabilitate the user’s mental state and it will lessen the stress or hassle in going to a psychologist every time and at the same time, it will be much safer for the client. Instead of waiting for your doctor to ask you what kind of difficulties the client is experiencing, how is the client’s day, the client can respond freely, they can draw, create a collage of photos, and they can create a diary. When the psychologist looks at their client’s responses, they can quickly provide evidenced-based psychotherapies.
